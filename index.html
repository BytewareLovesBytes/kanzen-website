Search for anime and manga

Use Kanzen to search for your favourite anime and manga. Kanzen provides watch links for all your favourites!

Powered by AniList
Our searching commands are powered by AniList, a popular anime tracking platform. Kanzen also gives you the ability to link your AniList account to your Discord account so that your friends can find you and other people can give you a follow!

Schedule anime releases

Use Kanzen to setup an anime releases channel so that you and your community knows when the latest anime episodes are released. Setup a channel for specific anime releases, or even all anime releases.

Powered by AnimeSchedule
Our release channels are powered by AnimeSchedule. AnimeSchedule is a popular site to check when the latest anime episodes are released.
